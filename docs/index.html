Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

LottoPilot ("we", "our", or "the app") is a lottery ticket checking and strategy tool. This policy describes how we handle your information.

1. Information We Collect

Camera & Photos: The app may access your camera and photo library to scan lottery tickets. Images are processed locally on your device. We do not upload or store scanned ticket images on our servers.

Location: We use your approximate location (region/country only) to show relevant lottery prize rules for Canada and the United States. We do not store your precise location.

Check History: Your ticket check results are stored locally on your device. If you sign in, records may sync to our backup service (Supabase) for your convenience. We do not sell this data.

Account: If you create an account, we store your email for authentication. This is handled by Supabase.

2. Data Storage

Most data is stored locally on your device. Optional cloud backup uses Supabase. Purchases are processed by Google Play; we do not store payment details.

3. Third-Party Services

We use:

  • Supabase – Authentication and optional data sync
  • Google Play – In-app purchases (Pirate Plan, Astronaut Plan)

Each service has its own privacy policy. We do not share your data with advertisers.

4. Data We Do Not Sell

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties.
